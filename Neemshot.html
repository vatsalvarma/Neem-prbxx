What’s Special About This Drink?

Neem has been respected in traditional wellness practices for centuries for its cleansing and balancing properties. Its naturally bitter taste is a sign of its strength — a reminder that powerful wellness often comes from simple, earthy ingredients.

Shake & Gulp Neem Shot makes this traditional ingredient easier to include in modern routines. Balanced with a touch of natural sweetness, it delivers the essence of neem in a convenient, drinkable form. Since natural extracts settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.
